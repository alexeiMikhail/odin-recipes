Ham Sandwich

Description

This is the easiest recipe you ever wanna make. A four year old could make this.

But just in case you're stuck I'm gonna give you a recipe.

Ingredients

  • bread of choice
  • lunchmeat of choice
  • toppings of choice
  • sauces of choice

Steps

  1. Remove two slices of bread from packaging and stage them on a plate or paper towel. If you don't have access to either, then you may stage directly on the countertop, but in that case you will have to wipe down your workspace at the end.
  2. Add sauces to the bread.
  3. Layer lunchmeat over the sauce on only one slice of bread. This will be your bottom bread.
  4. Layer toppings over the lunchmeat on your bottom bread.
  5. Flip the top bread upside down so that the saucy side is facing the bottom bread, and set it down squarely on top.
  6. Using your pocket knife, cut the sandwich diagonally into two triangular halves.
  7. Put it in your face hole and enjoy!
